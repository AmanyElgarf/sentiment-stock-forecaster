Sentiment Analyzer

Not only do we provide top headlines of a particular stock, we also provide a prediction on price trends on a stock of your choosing. This, in turn, can give you a piece of information to take into account when handling stocks.

First, you must type in the stock you're interested in. Next, in the backend, we use a webscraper to grab headlines about your stock. Finally, we calculate a sentiment score from the headlines--this score indicates whether the price trend of your stock is strong, weak, or stagnant.

Technical Forecaster

Rate of change measures the rate of change in 5 day increments and returns an unbounded percentage. Stochastic Oscillator measured the likelihood that a stock will fluctuate and returns a percentage from 0 to 100%. ASI measured the volatility and long term trend of a stock and returns a percentage from -100 to 100%

ARIMA predicts the short term stock price by fitting the past prices to a polynomial function. Fourier predicts the long term stock prices by fitting them to a periodic function.
